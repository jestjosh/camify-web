Camify

Privacy Policy

Effective June 26, 2026

Camify is a camera and photo editing app. This policy explains what information Camify collects, how it is used, and how users can control it.

Information We Collect

Camify may collect account information such as email address, username, sign-in provider, profile avatar, support messages, and subscription status. If you use the Wall of Fame or cloud-backed features, photos or edited images you choose to upload are stored so the feature can work.

Photos and Camera

Camify requests camera and photo library access only to let you take, import, edit, save, and share images. Images remain on your device unless you choose to upload them, post them to Wall of Fame, contact support with them, or use another sharing destination.

Face Data (AR Filters)

The optional AR Filters feature uses Apple's ARKit face tracking, which relies on the TrueDepth camera on supported devices. Camify uses this only to detect the position and geometry of your face in real time so decorative visual effects (such as a mesh, glasses, a crown, or stars) can be aligned to the camera preview while the AR screen is open.

This face data is processed live on your device and is used solely to render these on-screen effects. Camify does not save, upload, sell, share, or transmit ARKit face tracking data to Camify or to any third party, and it is never used for advertising, marketing, analytics, data mining, identification, or user profiling. The face-tracking data is held only transiently in memory during the session and is discarded when you leave the AR screen; no face model is retained. If you capture a photo while using AR Filters, only the resulting image is saved, and only to your device unless you separately choose to save it to your account, post it, or share it.

Purchases

Camify Pro purchases are handled by Apple and trusted subscription service providers. Camify receives purchase status so Pro features can be unlocked. Camify does not receive full payment card details.

Third-Party Services

Camify uses trusted service providers for authentication, storage, support tickets, and subscription status. Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In may also process data when you choose those sign-in options. These services process data needed to provide those features.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, maintain accounts, sync purchase status, support users, moderate reported Wall of Fame content, improve reliability, and comply with legal obligations.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the Camify app from Settings, then Edit Profile, then Delete Account. Deletion removes your account, profile, private photos, Wall of Fame posts, and avatar from Camify systems, except information we must retain for legal, security, or transaction records.

Contact

Email support@camify.cc for privacy questions or account help.
