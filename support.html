Support

Need help with Camify? Email support@camify.cc.

Account Deletion

Open Camify, go to Settings, choose Edit Profile, then tap Delete Account. This deletes your account, profile, private photos, Wall of Fame posts, and avatar.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions and refunds are managed by Apple. Open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions to manage Camify Pro.

Privacy
